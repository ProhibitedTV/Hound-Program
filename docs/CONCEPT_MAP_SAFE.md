# Concept Map

Goal: keep the design close to the original visual reference while using simple printable modules and common electronics envelopes.

Targets:

- long low body
- dark faceted panels
- side hub details
- paired leg links
- replaceable feet
- removable electronics tray

Working envelopes:

- standard servo body: 41 x 21 x 41 mm
- small servo body: 24 x 13 x 24 mm
- servo driver board: 63 x 26 x 8 mm
- controller board: 56 x 30 x 12 mm
- carbon tube: 10 mm or 12 mm OD
- M3 clearance: 3.4 mm
- M4 clearance: 4.5 mm
